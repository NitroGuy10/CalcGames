NitroGuy's CalcGames


What are CalcGames?

NitroGuy's CalcGames are short and simple games written in TI-BASIC. They can be played on TI-84+C (Silver Edition) and TI-84+CE graphing calculators.


How to Install Games

  1. Download TI Connect CE from here and set up according to instructions
  2. Connect your calculator to your computer via USB cable
  3. Open TI Connect CE and select "Calculator Explorer"
  4. Drag the .8xp file of the game you want into the explorer
  5. You are now free to disconnect you calculator
  6. To play: press [prgm], choose the game, and press [enter] twice
  7. Enjoy!
  8. (OPTIONAL) To protect your games from being deleted when your calculator's RAM is cleared, archive them when you are not playing them
  9. To archive/unarchive, press [2nd] [mem] [2] [7] and press [enter] to archive/unarchive the selected game (archived programs are marked with an asterisk)
